Testing Local Game Mode

Steps to test:

  1. Go to http://localhost:3000
  2. Click "Local Battle" or go to /game and select "Local Game"
  3. Configure game settings and click "Create Game"
  4. Try to move pieces - they should update immediately without refresh
  5. Verify both red and black players can move in turns

Expected Behavior:

  • ✅ Pieces should move immediately when dragged or clicked
  • ✅ Board should update without needing refresh
  • ✅ Turn should alternate between red and black
  • ✅ No multi-tab sync warnings in console
